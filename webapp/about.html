The Great Crop Database

Our site allows farmers to contribute and recieve help from the rest of the farming community.

There are three main parts to how our company opperates.

First, we issue devices to help farmers collect vital data about crops.

Second, we made a easy to use data collection form to gather information about various aspects of the crops.

Lastly, we provide insightful visualizations that allow you to gain a deeper insight in crop statistics.
